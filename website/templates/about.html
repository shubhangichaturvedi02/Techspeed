Technology Is For Everyone.

Over the last decade, there has been a dramatic increase in internet use among older adults. According to statistics Canada, individuals aged 60+ are the fastest growing population of internet users, with Internet uses more than doubling (32% to 71%) among this age group from 2007 to 2018. However it has also been found that nearly half of older adults require help with their devices. This reduced technology competency among older adults contributes to the fundamental societal issues of ‘Digital Divide’.

Due to observing the struggles of his grandparents using technology to keep in contact with loved ones during the pandemic, Divyansh & Shantam were looking for a way to combat social isolation among seniors during this difficult time. As a result, they started Techspeed, a non-profit which provides free technical Support tailored for older adults, with the primary goal of combatting social isolation by teaching older adults video chat platforms.

Although achieving great success, Shantam felt that there were opportunities to incorporate technology to improve the time-efficiency and range of customers that could receive support. With the help of local NGOs, that speed was born!

Techspeed is a marketplace for universal technical support and education. At Techspeed, we address all your technology needs by providing instantaneous video and written tutorials within the hour telephone and video chats technical support, and appointment-based 1 on 1 online lessons and monthly group online lessons. At Techspeed, we believe that everyone should be able to take advantage to technology to improve lives!

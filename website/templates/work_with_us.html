Become a Techspeed Techexpert


Want to help older adults use technology and improve their lives? As a Techspeed Tech expert you will be able to be your own boss, setting your own hours so you can provide support to seniors when most convenient for you! Responsibilities of a Tech Expert include:

  • Completing telephone and video chat technical support calls.
  • Conducting o 1-on-1 and group lessons covering common online platforms and computer topics.

Required credientials

In terms what we are looking for in a potential candidate, we require the following:

  • A strong technical background, preferably someone with IT or engineering experience/ education.
  • Strong problem solving communication teamwork skills.
  • A recent criminal background check (conducted in the past two years).
  • Minimum commitment of 5 hours per work.

Selection Process

Upon completion of the application form (see below), you will be contacted in 1-2 business days via email to let you know wether you are selected to be a Techspeed Tech expert.
